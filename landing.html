This website helps medical professionals segment patients using ultrasound scans of UTERINE FIRBOIDS more readily.

For more usability and accessibility, it also has a dedicated app..

About

The prevalence of uterine fibroids in Kenyan women is not well established. This website and application's goal is to close that knowledge gap and raise awareness of the problem by aiding in the detection and segmentation of ultrasound images of the same.

Accurate

The detection and segmentation of the Uterine Fibroids is tested to be 96% accurate

Cross Platform

It can be used on multiple Operating system such as Android, IOS etc...

Fast

The Segmentation process is fast and reliable to ease the work of Medical Personnel
